Eveytime I learn a new technology, I make it a point to implement it in atleast one project of significant size. Over time, I've ended up with a sizeable number of such projects of various scales, all of which I upload on my GitHub. Here are a few noted ones.

MediGo

MediGo is an App based Teleconsultation platform that aims to connect Users with Doctors over text, audio and video calls. It digitalizes the entire process of consultation by noting down the User's symptoms and preparing a Medical Report on basis of that, which is then sent to the Doctor. The Doctor can further manage his Patient Records, Clinic Data through the App and also provide Digital Prescriptions to the consulted patients.

AgroAcres

AgroAcres is a Smart Agriculture Platform built to educate Farmers on the modern Farming methods. With the use of a Crop Calender and Chatbot, it advises the Farmer on how to take care of his crop from the very sowing till the harvest. And with the in-built e-commerce plaform, Farmers and other Traders can put up their products for sale for better transparency in the whole process, thus getting fair prices.

Know More

Aasara

Aasara is an app based resource mapping and management system which was built in response to this COVID19 Crisis. It provides an e-commerce platform to connect users with the local shopkeepers in their area and purchase groceries, either through home deliveries or pickups. It consists of 2 Apps, one for the User to explore nearby groceries and place Orders. And the other for the Shopkeeper to register his Shop and start accepting Customers.

Know More

Gesture Keyboard

This is an IoT enabled device that uses Machine Learning to type out text/audio messages with hand gestures. The gesture keyboard module consists of an Arduino Nano micro-processor with an MPU-6050 attached to it. It captures the movement of the module through its 6 axes and compares it against the 1500+ samples that we have recorded beforehand and predicts the correct character. For this purpose We use pyGARL, a Python library which uses SVM algorithms.
